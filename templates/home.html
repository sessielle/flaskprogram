    What is the Wildlife genre?

    The wildlife genre is a genre of documentary films or film series that typically encompasses animals and plants, usually in their natural habitat. Nature documentary are typically informative and may feature footage of captive animals, their relationships with humans and the ecosystem and so on.

    Think National Geographic.

    What is the Wildcard genre?

    The wildcard genre is a completely made up term here at Films in Review. We use this genre to feature fun documentaries ranging from the human experience. These documentaries could be informative, historical, about the arts and humanities, or purely for entertainment.

    Think TED Talks.
